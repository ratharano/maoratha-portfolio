---
title: Clarity over features
date: 2025-03-10
tags:
  - design
  - product
---

The best interfaces I ship are the ones that remove a decision, not add a toggle.

When a team asks for "one more setting," I ask what job the user is hiring the product to do in the next thirty seconds. If the answer is still fuzzy, the problem is positioning—not UI.

Minimal does not mean empty. It means every element earns its place in the hierarchy.
