## Overview

Creator Lab combined curriculum design, community rituals, and a minimal product surface so creators could ship without drowning in tooling.

## Approach

- Mapped learner journeys before visual design
- Built a component library in plain CSS for fast iteration
- Ran quarterly usability reviews with paying members

## Outcome

The platform became the anchor offer for the studio, with retention driven by clarity—not feature count.
