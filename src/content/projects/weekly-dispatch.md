## Overview

A long-running newsletter treated as a product: readable on mobile, skimmable in two minutes, dense with signal.

## Approach

- Designed a repeatable editorial template
- Tuned subject lines and preview text with reader interviews
- Kept subscribe flow to a single field, no funnel

## Outcome

Open rates stayed above 40% for three years—proof that restraint and voice beat automation.
